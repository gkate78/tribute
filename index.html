Venerable Aloysius Schwartz

(1930 - 1992)

Fr. Al's life

  • September 18, 1930 - Birth of John Philip Aloysius Schwartz. Born in Washington, D.C., third among eight children of Louis F. Schwartz and Cedelia A. Bourassa.
  • June 29, 1957 - Priesthood Ordination. Ordained as a diocesan priest in St. Martin's Church, Washington, D.C. by Bishop McNamara, Auxiliary Bishop of Washington.
  • December 8, 1957 - Arrived in South Korea, where he was incardinated in the Diocese of Busan South Korea.
  • August 15, 1964 - The Sisters of Mary religious congregation was founded in Busan Korea. It was originally called the Mariahwe Sisters and currently consists of more than 400 Sisters who operate charitable programs in the Philippines, South Korea, Mexico, Guatemala, Brazil, Honduras, and Tanzania.
  • 1989 - Diagnosed with Lou Gehrig’s disease, Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis(ALS).
  • March 16, 1992 - Death of Fr. Al.
  • December 10, 2003 - Opening of the cause for Beatification and Canonization.
  • January 22, 2015 - Pope Francis ordered the promulgation of the Decree of the Heroic Virtues of Fr. Al.
